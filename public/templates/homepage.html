Welcome to TTC Ninja!

The purpose of this website is to help you find information about your next TTC bus or streetcar arrivals.

To find out when your next bus or streetcar will arrive click on the Lookup tool link in the menu above. From there the process should be simple.

  1. Find your bus route. Enter the route number or the route name in the search box. Then click on the correct route on the right hand side of the page.
  2. Find your bus stop. Enter the stop name in the search box and click on the correct bus stop on the right hand side of the page.
  3. Your next bus arrivals. The next arrivals at this stop should now be displayed on the right hand of the page.
